Privacy Policy

We are here to outline our privacy policy for the website regarding how Lead2Money, collects, discloses, uses, and transfers your information.
We respect the privacy of each user and are dedicated to safeguarding it in every aspect.

User Information:
Currently, we don’t gather any dynamic user information in the background. Only details submitted through contact forms are collected and securely protected for the user’s benefit.

Cookies:
On occasion, we may use cookies to enhance site responsiveness. Cookies cannot extract data from a hard drive, and private information remains untouched and safeguarded.

Links to Third-Party Sites:Articles may contain links to third-party sites governed by their respective privacy policies. Once you leave our servers, the information you provide is subject to the policies of the site you are visiting.
Accessing and Updating Personal information Lead2Money takes due care and allows users the opportunity to edit and correct information. We ensure proper user identification before granting access to amend personal information.

If you delete your account:
You can't regain access to your account. It may take up to 30 days from the beginning of the deletion process to delete your Lead2money information. Copies of your information may also remain after the 30 days in the backup storage that we use to recover in the event of a disaster, software error, or other data loss event. Your information isn't available to you on lead2money application during this time.
We may retain certain log data in our databases even after you delete your account, but such data will be disassociated from any identifying information such that it can no longer be linked to your account. We also replace any instance of your account ID with a replacement identifier which can't be linked back to your account once it is deleted. We may also keep your information for things like legal issues, terms violations, or harm prevention efforts. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more information.
